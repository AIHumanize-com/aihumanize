Perplexity is a measure of how unpredictable a text is and how likely it is to confuse or perplex the average reader. AI language models strive to produce texts with low perplexity

Sentence Length indicates the average number of words per sentence in a text. Shorter sentences may suggest human writing, while unusually long or uniform sentences can be a marker of AI-generated content.

Length Variation reflects the diversity of sentence lengths in text. Higher variation suggests human writing, while low variation may indicate AI generation.

Length Normality scores text's adherence to typical sentence length patterns. Higher scores could suggest AI generation, as AI tends to closely follow these norms, while lower scores often indicate human authorship..
